Wikidata Class Hierarchy Visualization

Wikidata is an open knowledge base with a class hierarchy via the subclass property.

Class hierarchies are useful as they convey taxonomical information and properties inherited by subclasses.

This application focuses on conveying transitive and sibling relationships in the Wikidata class hierarchy.

The data visualized here are a microscopic slice of Wikidata.

Usage Instructions

The header at the top of the page contains classes that we can browse the subclasses of. Click them to explore them.

Blue dots are dots with subclasses. Click on them to expand them and explore their subclasses. Click on them again to collapse them.

Yellow dots are classes with no subclasses.

Hover over the dots or links and see their information in the sidebar.

Scroll to zoom, drag & drop dots, or click and drag to pan as necessary.
