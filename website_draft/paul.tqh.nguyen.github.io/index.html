Background & Bio

My name is Paul Nguyen.

I am a software engineer with industry experience in data integration, natural language processing, and knowledge graph engineering.

I am currently the Data Integration Technical Lead and Member of the Technical Staff at Cycorp.

I earned my Master of Science degree at the University of Virginia under Connelly Barnes.

I earned my Bachelor of Science degree at Washington & Lee University where I worked with Joshua Stough.

Outside of the tech world, I worked as a swing dance instructor for several years under the Jazz Dance Education Council. In Austin, you might find me teaching at Four on the Floor.
